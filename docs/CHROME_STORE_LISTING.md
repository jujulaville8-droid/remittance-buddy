# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Compare remittance rates instantly. Find the cheapest way to send money to the Philippines with AI-powered recommendations.

## Detailed Description
Remittance Buddy helps you find the best deal when sending money to the Philippines. Compare rates from Wise, Remitly, Western Union, Xoom, MoneyGram, WorldRemit, and Pangea — all in one click.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Click the extension icon
2. Enter how much you want to send
3. See every provider ranked by total cost, speed, and delivery method
4. Click to send with the winning provider

FEATURES
- Instant rate comparison across 7+ providers
- AI chat assistant that helps you decide based on your priorities
- GCash, bank deposit, and cash pickup options compared
- Shows exactly how many pesos your recipient gets
- Highlights savings vs the most expensive option
- English and Tagalog support

WHO IS THIS FOR
- Overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) sending money home
- Anyone sending USD to PHP regularly
- People tired of checking multiple provider websites

FREE TO USE
Remittance Buddy is completely free. We earn a small commission from providers when you use our links — this never affects the rates you receive or our recommendations.

PRIVACY
- We never access your browsing history
- We only communicate with our own servers
- Your data is never sold to third parties
- Read our full privacy policy: https://remittancebuddy.com/privacy

## Permissions Justification (for Chrome review)

### sidePanel
Used to display the AI chat assistant in Chrome's side panel. Users can ask questions about remittance providers, get personalized recommendations, and compare rates through a conversational interface.

### storage
Used to save the user's authentication token, language preference (English/Tagalog), last-used transfer amount, and locally cached rate comparison data. All data is stored locally on the user's device.

### alarms
Used to periodically refresh cached exchange rates (every 30 minutes) and sync anonymous usage analytics (every 15 minutes) so the popup opens with up-to-date information.

### notifications
Reserved for future use — will notify users when exchange rates change significantly for their preferred corridor. Not currently active.

### host_permissions (remittancebuddy.com)
The extension communicates exclusively with our own API server at remittancebuddy.com to fetch rate comparisons, power the AI chat assistant, and sync analytics. No other websites are accessed.
